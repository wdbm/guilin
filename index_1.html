guilin

a fluid CSS layout system

abstract

Guilin is fluid CSS layout system, designed to be simple to use for responsive layouts.

name

Guilin is named after the precinct of Guilin in the town of Bukit Batok in Singapore. The area is known for its HDB public flats, the layouts of which tend to be thoroughly planned. The park at Guilin is known as Little Guilin because it has rock features from a granite quarry that resemble the granite rock formations in the city of Guilin in China. The name means "Forest of Sweet Osmanthus".
